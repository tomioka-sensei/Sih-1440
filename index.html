About Us

SahayakSetu is a platform build to provide intercommunication among various disaster management agencies.

Real Time collabration with resource sharing

Welcome to our web app, where disaster response becomes a collective effort. We are dedicated to facilitating collaboration among various disaster agencies, fostering a sense of unity when it's needed most. In times of crisis, our platform serves as a hub where organizations can seamlessly share critical resources, expertise, and data to optimize response efforts. Together, we empower disaster response teams to work harmoniously, ensuring that aid reaches those in need swiftly and efficiently. With a shared commitment to resilience and preparedness, we are here to make the world a safer place.

In addition to facilitating collaboration among disaster agencies, our web app empowers individuals to be part of the solution. With just a few clicks, you can apply to volunteer during emergencies, allowing you to respond to SOS calls in the shortest time possible. We believe that every helping hand counts, and our platform streamlines the process, connecting willing volunteers with urgent needs swiftly and efficiently. Together, we can make a difference when it matters most.

Frequently Asked Questions

This app/website is designed to facilitate the sending of disaster management alerts by users to relevant authorities and the community.

To report a disaster or emergency, simply open the app/website and follow the prompts to submit information about the incident, including its location, nature, and severity.

You can report a wide range of disasters and emergencies, including natural disasters (e.g., earthquakes, floods, wildfires), accidents, public health crises, and more.

Alerts submitted through this platform are typically received by relevant local authorities, emergency services, and community responders, depending on the nature and location of the incident.
